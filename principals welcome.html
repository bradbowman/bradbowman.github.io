PRINCIPALS WELCOME

    

    
    	

	


	
It is my pleasure to welcome you to Koonibba School website. Our school is a special place to be, providing excellent teaching and facilities for academic pursuits, sports, music and the arts. Comprehensive information about Koonibba Aboriginal School is included here and I encourage you to take a tour of our school through this website.


We are a truly dynamic, vibrant and globally engaged school located on the West Coast of South Australia. Our school has a successful tradition of innovative learning practices and excellence in many areas. Our staff take great pride in providing a safe and secure learning environment, whilst at the same time providing a full range of dynamic and challenging academic and extracurricular programs.


Koonibba Aboriginal School fosters a community of respectful, resilient and responsible learners. This is achieved by our trademark standards of being dynamic, unified and working with integrity. Our mission is to engage every child and student so that they achieve at the highest possible level of their learning and wellbeing, through quality care and teaching.


Regular updates of this website will keep visitors, and particularly current and prospective parents, in touch with the latest news and endeavours of school life at Koonibba Aboriginal School.


Mr Wade Branford

Principal
