It gives me great pleasure as Principal of Koonibba Aboriginal School to welcome you to our school’s website. KAS website has been developed to enable parents, students, visitors and the wider school community to become familiar with our school, its policies and procedures.
  At Koonibbs Aboriginal School we encourage a strong partnership between home and school. We endeavour to work cooperatively together with our families to support student learning, wellbeing and achievement. We believe that parents are their child’s first teachers and that education continues throughout life which means that education is not the sole responsibility of school. We value parent participation and encourage you to take part in the many formal and informal programs and activities that we offer.
