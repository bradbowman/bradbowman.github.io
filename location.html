LOCATION

    

    
    	


Koonibba is an Aboriginal Community in South Australia, located 45KM west of Ceduna.  To get to our school you need to take National Highway 1 heading West towards Penong from Ceduna, you travel 40KM's on National Highway 1, when you see the sign saying 45KM to Penong you turn right in 500 metres and travel 5KM until you reach the Koonibba Community, further details in the map below.
